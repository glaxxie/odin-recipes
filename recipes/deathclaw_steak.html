Deathclaw steak

Description:

A traditional Deathclaws steak, simple but powerful dish. You have to have a death wish to even think about making this yourself.

Ingredients:

  • Deathclaws tenderloin (Goodluck!)
  • Radaway
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Mentats (any flavor you want)
  • Glowing fungus (Optional, asparagus alternative)

Directions:

  1. Prepping the meat by soaking them in Radaway for at least 12 hours.
  2. Cut the meat into pieces of 1.5 - 2 inches thick, add salt and pepper and let it rest for 6 hours
  3. Boil Mutshoot fungus with Mentats for half an hour
  4. Grilled the meat for 15 mins with your best flamthrower
  5. Let the meat cool down for 3-5 mins, then serve with Glowing fungus
